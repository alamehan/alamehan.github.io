Let's go through the five stages of the Design Thinking process to come up with a solution for the challenge of creating a more engaging online education platform.

    1. Empathize: Understand the Users

    • Surveys: Distribute questionnaires to existing and potential learners asking about their learning preferences, challenges with current platforms, and what they'd love to see.

    • Interactive Challenges: Gamify learning with quizzes, real-world tasks, and puzzles.
